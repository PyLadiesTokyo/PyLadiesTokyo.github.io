Global PyLadies Organization¶

The global PyLadies community has no formal organization behind it; no board of directors, no appointed leadership. We found that PyLadies growth and success stems from being decentralized and non-bureaucratic.

Contact¶

We do have a few point people that can answer and address general, or non-location specific PyLadies questions. If you are not a local PyLadies leader, please check out Contact. If you are a local PyLadies leader, please take a look at Global PyLadies Community.
